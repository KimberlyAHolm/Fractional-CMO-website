Community Impact Report

\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0

The station's first Community Impact Report was created to bring greater visibility to PBS Western Reserve's mission, community value, and regional impact. The report synthesizes the organization's work across education, programming, community outreach, and local storytelling into a clear, accessible narrative for donors, partners, government officials, and the public.

The report has since become a cornerstone of the station's annual storytelling and engagement strategy.

Social Media Growth Through Content Strategy

\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0
\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0 \'a0

Improving the effectiveness of social media began with building a stronger internal foundation and a strategy grounded in defined content pillars. Working collaboratively with the Social Media and Community Outreach Specialist, content pillars were established that aligned with strategic priorities: local and national programming promotion, station updates including events and community partnerships, and locally relevant user-generated content.

\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0 \'a0

The impact was substantial. Facebook followers grew more than fivefold, reach expanded from thousands to over one million, and engagement increased across reactions, shares, and comments. Instagram and LinkedIn also saw steady growth. Monetization features were added on Meta, X, and YouTube, and a YouTube Shorts strategy was launched to drive viewers to long-form content, streaming, and broadcast.

Hiram College Awareness Campaigns

\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0
\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0

One of the first goals when joining Hiram College was to elevate awareness. The College had not had an active advertising campaign in more than a decade. The majority of high school students in Northeast Ohio were not familiar with the College or its location. Two back-to-back multifaceted campaigns helped make Hiram College appear like it was everywhere.

\cf0 \cb1 \ \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0
\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0
\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0 \'a0

The campaigns relied heavily on digital advertising through social media and paid search on the Google Display Network. Campaign visuals and messaging were deliberately different from the typical college campaign \'97 instead of focusing on facilities and programs, Hiram's story was told through its students, focusing on experiences and outcomes with bold, vibrant imagery.

\cf0 \cb1 \ \cf2 \cb3 \'a0 \'a0 \'a0

Stories were introduced in a full-length recruitment video, edited into 15-second digital spots running on Google, YouTube, and TV in the Cleveland and Youngstown DMAs. Digital ads directed visitors to a landing page where they could learn more about each student before being invited to share contact information for follow-up nurturing by email, virtual tour, campus visit, or admission counselor conversation.

The community health report is an annual publication targeted to community leaders and government officials to share the story of how Summa Health System contributes to the health and wellness of the communities it serves. A vital publication that helps justify the health system's not-for-profit status.
