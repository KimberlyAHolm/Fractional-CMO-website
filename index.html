My Approach

How I work, and why it matters.

Serve the mission, move the numbers.

Strategy that isn't connected to what your organization is trying to accomplish isn't strategy — it's activity. I start with your mission and work outward from there, making sure every marketing decision has a reason behind it.

Build capacity, not dependency.

My goal is to leave your team stronger than I found it. I hire, coach, and mentor the people around me. I build systems that work without me in the room. The measure of good fractional leadership is what stays when the engagement ends.

Outcomes over deliverables.

A campaign that launches on time and misses the goal is not a success. I hold myself accountable to results — awareness, pipeline, enrollment, loyalty, advocacy — not just the production of work product.

Integrity is non-negotiable.

I'll tell you what I actually think. If your strategy needs rethinking, I'll say so. If a vendor isn't performing, I'll name it. You deserve a partner who gives you an honest read, not just a comfortable one.
